Which month of pregnancy is good for photoshoot? or Which week is best for maternity photoshoot?

Usually, maternity photo shoots are scheduled between weeks 28 and 36 of pregnancy. While you’re still somewhat comfortable and mobile, your baby bump is often round and noticeable during this stage. This window reduces the possibility of discomfort or early labor while providing the best possible vision of the belly.
